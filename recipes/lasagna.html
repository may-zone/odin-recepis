Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or ground chicken (or vegetables for a veggie version)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Salt, black pepper, and Italian herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk, nutmeg)
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese (optional)
  • Mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)

How to Make Lasagna :

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground meat (or vegetables) and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and Italian herbs. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  6. Prepare the béchamel sauce: melt butter in a pan, whisk in flour, then gradually add milk while stirring until thickened. Season with salt and a pinch of nutmeg.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then a layer of lasagna sheets..
  8. Add a layer of béchamel sauce and sprinkle with ricotta/cottage cheese (optional) and mozzarella.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  11. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
