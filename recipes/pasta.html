Calzone is an Italian folded pizza, typically stuffed with cheese, meats, and vegetables, then baked until golden.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 200g cheese
  • 100g mushrooms

How to Make Calzone :

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out pizza dough into a round shape.
  3. Place fillings (such as mozzarella, ricotta, pepperoni, ham, mushrooms, or vegetables) on one half of the dough.
  4. Fold the other half over to cover the fillings and press the edges firmly to seal.
  5. Brush the top with olive oil or egg wash.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with marinara sauce for dipping.
