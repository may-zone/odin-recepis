Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made from wheat and water, often served with a variety of sauces and toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, or any type you like)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (chopped or minced)
  • Onion (optional)
  • Tomato sauce or fresh tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (for garnish)

How to Make Pasta :

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook the pasta until al dente (about 8–10 minutes).
  2. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic (and onion if you like) until fragrant..
  4. Add tomato sauce or chopped fresh tomatoes, season with salt and black pepper, and simmer for 10–15 minutes
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil on top.
